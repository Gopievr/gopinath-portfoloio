Bio.

I study how spatially ranked ethnic structures shape access to opportunity, public goods, and well‑being. I combine formal theory with causal and descriptive empirical methods, drawing on administrative data, historical archives, and fieldwork. I am currently a Doctoral Candidate in the Public Policy area at the Indian Institute of Management Bangalore.
